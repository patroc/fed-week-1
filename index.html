Biography

Patrice Roc is a graphic designer born and raised in Haiti and currently based in Toronto. With a fervent interest in the world of art and design, Roc completed the 'infographisme' course at College Ahuntsic and eventually went on to procure full-time and freelance design work for a number of organizations ranging from event to pharmaceutical companies.
Driven by his interests in film and music, Roc has worked on a number of products such as posters, flyers, brochures, invitations, business cards, CD covers and other promotional material related to the exhibition of music and film work. Most recently, Roc has expanded his area of expertise to include web design and has successfully completed a number of websites for charity projects in his hometown, Port-au-Prince as well as for companies marketing green technology. When not working on a new design project, Roc spends his time composing and producing commissioned soundtracks for short and feature-length films as a personal hobby.
